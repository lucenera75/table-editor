INCENTIVE PAY

Strategic Pay endorses the use of incentives for senior positions when they are structured to drive and reward decisions and behaviours that help achieve the organisation’s stretch goals and objectives.

Incentives typically include both targets and caps, expressed as a percentage of base pay. Payment caps (i.e. the maximum that will be paid per annum) are typically used to control the costs of variable pay, and to:

  • Avoid windfall payments
  • Limit earnings
  • Manage risk
  • Ensure board approval
